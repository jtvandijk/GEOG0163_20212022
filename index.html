Seminar preparation

The activity for this week's seminar will consist of interrogating technology in a localised context (e.g. dating apps, transport aggregation apps, virtual assistant AI technology, facial recognition software, period trackers, fitness activity trackers, welfare tech for COVID tracing and 'e-governance', food delivery apps such as Deliveroo, Zomato, social media etc.) and how their local implementation results in particular (re)productions of social/political relations.

In preparation for the seminar, please:

  1. Come to the seminar prepared with one example of a piece of technology or smartphone app, to discuss how this particular technology/app (re)produces social/political relations when used or implemented in a localised context.
  2. Read the following short article: Han, K. 2021. Broken promises: How Singapore lost trust on contact tracing privacy. Online article. [Link]

You do not have to submit anything, however, do come prepared as this will be guiding our discussion.
